Industry Collaboration

CTS Group are delighted to be a Visionary member of the iMasons Climate Accord(iCA)

This global coalition of digital infrastructure companies are working together to cut carbon emissions from data center power, materials, and equipment.

It develops open standards, reporting frameworks, and practical tools to help members measure and reduce carbon.

Progress is driven through international Working Groups, each focused on specific, time-bound goals set by the iCA Governing Body.
