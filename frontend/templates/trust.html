Trust

Our carbon accounting system is designed to be transparent and auditable, providing clear, reliable, and verifiable emissions data. The system is built on a foundation of structured data workflows, traceability, and controlled calculation logic, ensuring accuracy and consistency in emissions measurements.

Structured Data Workflows

The system is built on a foundation of structured data workflows, ensuring that data is collected in a consistent and compliant manner.

Traceability

Each data point is tracked and documented, providing a clear trail of how emissions are calculated and reported.

Controlled Calculation Logic

The system includes controlled calculation logic to ensure that emissions are calculated in a consistent and compliant manner.

Audit Ready Outputs

The system produces audit ready outputs, providing a clear and transparent view of emissions data. The outputs are designed to be easy to understand and use, and are formatted in a way that is easy to audit.
