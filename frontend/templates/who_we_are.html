Who We Are

{# EDIT: hero subtitle #}

At CTS Group, sustainability and ESG is about leadership and progress, not just compliance. We are committed to embedding environmental, social, and governance (ESG) principles across our operations, projects, and decision-making to create lasting value for our clients, our people, and our communities. We are one of the biggest desing and build contractors in Europe and the largest in the Nordics. With a globally diverse team, group companies and experienced partners, we use standardised solutions to build Data Centers.
