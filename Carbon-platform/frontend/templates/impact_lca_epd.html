Life Cycle Assessment & Environmental Product Declaration

We completed a full Life Cycle Assesment (LCA) of our prefabricated NordicEPOD to measure its environmental impact.

The LCA followed ISO 14044:2006 and is independetly verified to ensure accuracy and credibility

An Environmental Product Declaration (EPD) was developed in line with ISO 14025:2010, EN 21930, and 15804:2012+A2:2019, carried out by Asplan Viak, assessed emboided carbon across the product's life cycle, focusing on material extraction, manufacturing, and transport (A1-A3).

Verification by an accredited third party, will confirm compliance with global standards.
